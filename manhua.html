BL Manhua

05-Feb-2020 | Admin | Manhua

"When the Stars Align" is a manhua that explores the dynamics of a young love between an Alpha and an Omega in a futuristic Omegaverse. The story delves into the challenges and complexities of their relationship as they navigate life in different cities and attend different schools. The manga is known for its portrayal of deep emotional connections and the struggles of young love, making it a popular choice among fans of BL manga.

"Heaven Official's Blessing" is a manhua that has been adapted into a webtoon on WEBTOON, offering a digital comic reading experience. The story follows Xie Lian, the Crown Prince of the Xian Le kingdom, who ascended to the Heavens at a young age but faced challenges and demotion after achieving immortality and becoming a Martial God. Eight hundred years later, he ascends again, this time with no followers or incense burned in his name. The webtoon is available for free and provides a new online reading experience for fans of the series.

"Seize My Boyfriend" is a manhua that follows the story of a top-tier White Lotus character who uses a pure and innocent appearance to manipulate others' hearts through transmigration. The narrative involves the reunion of a couple after separation, the seduction of various characters, including the Film Emperor and overbearing Emperors, who become infatuated with him. The plot explores themes of seduction and tactfulness, with multiple storylines that keep the narrative engaging. The manhua is an adaptation of a novel titled "The White Lotus Is Online and Teaching" and has 179 chapters released so far,

MDZS, or The Grandmaster of Demonic Cultivation, is a popular manhua that follows the story of Wei WuXian, a powerful figure in demonic cultivation who was once revered but betrayed and killed by powerful clans. After his death, he is reincarnated into the body of a man abandoned by his clan and taken by Lan WangJi, a renowned cultivator. The series explores their complex relationship as they navigate a world filled with chaos and mystery, engaging in battles against monsters and uncovering the secrets of their past. The manhua has been adapted into a successful anime and live-action series, further popularizing its story.

"I Have to Be a Great Villain" is a BL manhua that follows the story of Wang Yi, a determined villain who is ruthless, evil, and a troublemaker. However, his plans are thwarted when the male protagonist, Qin, can read his inner thoughts, leading to humorous and unexpected situations. The manga is available for reading on various platforms, including VyManga and Bato.To, and is known for its clever plotting, dramatic tension, and chemistry between its male leads.
