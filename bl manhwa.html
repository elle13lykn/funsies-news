"Obey Me" is a manhwa that has captivated readers with its intense drama and emotional depth. The story follows Jinyoo, a character who has become a hermit due to an abusive father and a traumatic bullying experience. His life takes a turn when he is kidnapped by his high school classmate, Gyuh-wool, who has developed a twisted obsession over him. The manhwa is praised for its breathtaking art and emotional storytelling, making it a must-read for fans of the genre.

"Fruit of the Tongue" is a Horror Psychological Webtoon that falls under the Yaoi genre. The story follows Noah, a character who was met six years ago in an orphanage. His closed-off appearance made the reader feel like they were seeing their younger self, leading to a desire to care for him more. The manhwa explores themes of care, guidance, and the complexities of human relationships.

"Placebo: Let's Play" is an ongoing manhwa written and illustrated by Oi Binu, released in 2023. It is published in Lezhin Comics Webtoon and continues the short story "Placebo" from 2021. The series follows Seo Woo-yeon, who wants a simple university experience away from his annoying step-brother, Woo-jin. However, Woo-jin joins Woo-yeon in the same university, major, and classes, leading to a series of comedic and psychological twists.

Mr. A's Farm follows Little A, who travels to a rural farm to reunite with his uncle, only to discover the place eerily silent and his uncle missing. Upon arrival, he finds a chilling bible detailing how to care for livestock, which are actually human-like beings. Little A is thrust into the role of acting farm manager, managing a facility that produces livestock products using these creatures, while dark secrets plague the estate. The story explores themes of identity, survival, and the complexities of managing a unique farm.

Netkama Punch!!! is a webtoon that follows the story of Kim Seyeong, a male who portrays himself as female online. After reencountering Shin Heejae, the person who destroyed his gaming career in "Arcadia" a year prior, Seyeong decides to become a female character in the game to get Heejae to fall for him and humiliate him out of the game forever. However, the challenge of being a male trying to become the girlfriend of his worst enemy is a difficult one, especially when Heejae shows too much interest in him. The series is written by Bongyee and illustrated by Gongben, and it has been published by JAYPLE MEDIA since its first release in 2022.

"Dreadful Night" is an ongoing webtoon written and illustrated by Moan, first released in 2023. It has been published by Lezhin US and Bomtoom and currently has three seasons. The story follows Bada, a character trapped in a horror RPG who must find a way out by playing the game. As he navigates through the game's challenges, he encounters Nam Doha, an NPC who becomes a significant part of his journey. The webtoon explores themes of survival, identity, and the horrors of a game that seems to have no end.

"A Tree Without Roots" is a poignant manhwa that explores the complexities of Kwon Hee-seo’s life after the sudden suicide of his mother. The story follows Hee-seo as he searches for answers and the mysterious supporter who has secretly guided him. The manhwa delves into themes of loss, obsession, identity, and the search for deeper connections. It is written and illustrated by Lapo and has been published by Lezhin Comics Webtoon.

The story follows Kim Euihyun, an Omega who has reached his emotional limits and contemplates ending his life while holding his young sibling. At the brink of despair, he is rescued by Yeo Taeju, an Alpha and his debt collector, who saves both Euihyun and his sibling from the sea. Taeju offers Euihyun money, a house, and a job to help him recover from his debts, but this arrangement also introduces a complicated dynamic fueled by attraction and emotional tension.
