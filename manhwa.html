The Mad Demon, Jaha Lee, is the central protagonist of the manhwa "Return of the Mad Demon." After falling off a cliff while fleeing from the Demonic Cult for stealing the Jade of Heaven, Jaha awakens to find himself back in his 20s. With his knowledge of the future and martial arts, he aims to seek revenge and make a positive change in his life. The manhwa explores themes of revenge, martial arts, and the pursuit of dreams, all set against the backdrop of a fantasy world.

"I Became the Male Lead's Adopted Daughter" is a manhwa created by Latine. The story follows Duke Phileo Borreoti, who adopts a child from an orphanage, giving her the name of a 'beast' that only direct descendants of the family can receive. This decision leads to a complex relationship between the Duke and the adopted daughter, who is portrayed as having a corrupted innocence and a stepmother with a peculiar secret. The manhwa explores themes of power dynamics, family dynamics, and the challenges of growing up under the influence of a powerful figure.

The manhwa "Return of the Mount Hua Sect" follows the story of Chung Myung, the 13th Disciple of the Mount Hua Sect, who is one of the three great swordsmen and the Plum Blossom Sword Saint. He defeats Chun Ma, who brought destruction and disarray to the world. After the battle, Chung Myung breathes his last breath on top of the headquarter mountain of the Heavenly Demon Sect and is reborn after 100 years in the body of a child. The plot revolves around Chung Myung's journey to restore Mount Hua to its former glory and confront the challenges that come with it.

The "Greatest Estate Developer" universe is rich with prequels and related series that expand on the story of Suho Kim and his adventures. The prequel series, "Greatest Estate Developer (The World's Best Engineer)," is set thousands of years later and follows Kim Jang Cheol, an agricultural science student who transmigrates into an MMORPG and becomes the last boss. This series connects to the main plot of "The Greatest Estate Developer" and provides a deeper look into the world and characters involved.
