Hitorijime My Hero is a Japanese yaoi manga series that revolves around the romantic relationships between a high school teacher, Kousuke Ooshiba, and his student, Masahiro Setagawa, as well as Kousuke's younger brother and his childhood friend. Masahiro is a vulnerable teenager often bullied, but his life changes when he meets Kousuke, who becomes a protective figure in his life. The series explores themes of love, protection, and personal growth as Masahiro begins to see Kousuke as his "hero".

The series begins with Atsushi Nakajima, who is kicked out of his orphanage and finds himself starving by a river. In a twist of fate, he saves Osamu Dazai, a detective who was attempting suicide. Dazai, along with his partner Kunikida Doppo, works for the Armed Detective Agency, which specializes in handling cases involving individuals with supernatural abilities. Atsushi soon discovers that he possesses the ability to transform into a white tiger during the full moon, a power that led to his expulsion from the orphanage. As Atsushi joins the agency, he becomes embroiled in various conflicts, particularly with the Port Mafia, a criminal organization led by Ōgai Mori. Throughout the series, Atsushi encounters other ability users, including Ryūnosuke Akutagawa, a member of the Port Mafia, and Kyōka Izumi, a young girl forced to commit murder. The narrative explores themes of identity, belonging, and the moral complexities of using one's abilities.

Yarichin Bitch Club is an anime series that follows the story of Toono, a first-year student who transfers from Tokyo to an all-boys boarding school called Mori Moori Private School. He joins the photography club, which is humorously nicknamed the Yarichin Bitch Club, filled with colorful seniors. The club is not just a photography club; it also involves various interactions and complications between its members, particularly between Toono and another student named Yaguchi. The series was released in two OVAs from September 21, 2018, to August 17, 2019.

Mairimashita! Iruma-kun is a Japanese manga series by Osamu Nishi that follows Iruma Suzuki, a 14-year-old human boy who is sold by his irresponsible parents to a demon named Sullivan. After being adopted as his grandson, Iruma is enrolled in the Babyls School for Demons, where he navigates life in a prestigious demon school while concealing his true identity as a human. The series explores themes of adaptability, friendship, and personal growth as Iruma learns to embrace his new life and form relationships with fellow demons and other characters. The story has been adapted into an anime series, which has received positive reception for its faithful representation of the manga's humor and emotional depth.
